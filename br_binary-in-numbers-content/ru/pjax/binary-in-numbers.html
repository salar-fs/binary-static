Binary.com в цифрах

Binary.com - ведущая мировая компания по торговле бинарными опционами, работающая с 2000 года.

Активные клиенты

Our active client base exceeded 127,000 clients in 2019.

Количество контрактов

Our customers bought more than 275 million contracts on Binary.com in 2019, bringing the total number of contracts sold to over 1 billion since commencing business.

Объём покупок

In 2019, Binary.com generated more than USD 1.5 billion in turnover, bringing our total turnover since our inception to over USD 7.1 billion.

Число сотрудников и внештатных работников

We've grown over the years - both in human resources and offices. Currently, 250 employees are spread across four offices in Malaysia, Malta, Paraguay and the UAE. We're expecting to expand even more to support the demand for our products and services.
